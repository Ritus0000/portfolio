# 3D Rendering and Visual Systems

## Purpose

Show visual strength: rendering, materials, lighting, surfaces, scenes, environments, and game/real-time-adjacent visual thinking.

## What Belongs Here

- strong still renders
- material studies
- environment concepts
- sci-fi or technical scenes
- procedural or structured visual systems
- before/after material or lighting comparisons

## What This Proves

- spatial and visual taste
- ability to build believable scenes
- understanding of materials, lighting, and composition
- transferability toward technical art, 3D pipelines, simulation, and environment work

## Notes

This section should be visual-first. Code is optional. The important thing is to show control over atmosphere, surface, scale, and visual clarity.
